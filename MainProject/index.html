Recipes

Golden Castle Meal

Description

  1. The first recipe you're going to learn is from the manga Delicious in Dungeon/Dungeon Meshi
  2. It's called "Golden Castle Meal" and it's made of vegetables, slime, minotaur meat and some other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables in Slime Aspic
  • Beef Rib Steak (from 5 year old Minotaur)
  • Blade Fish Loaf
  • Potato and Rabbit Soup

Adeptus' Temptation

Description

  1. The second recipe you're going to learn is from the game Genshin Impact
  2. It's called "Adeptus' Temptation" and it's made of ham, crab, shrimp and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 4 Ham
  • 3 Crab
  • 3 Shrimp Meat
  • 3 Matsutake

Exorcism Sorbet

Description

  1. The third recipe you're going to learn is also from the manga Delicious in Dungeon/Dungeon Meshi
  2. It's called "Exorcism Sorbet" and it's made of ghosts and some other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml Special Interfaith Holy Water
  • 1 Sturdy jar
  • 1 Sacred cordon rope(if not available use regular rope)
  • 5 Ghosts
